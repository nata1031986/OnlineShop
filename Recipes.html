Moisturizing Face Cream

  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil;
  • 1 tablespoon of aloe vera gel;
  • 5 drops of lavender essential oil;
  • 2 tablespoons of shea butter.

Instructions:

  • Melt shea butter and coconut oil together;
  • Let it cool for a few min,add aloe vera gel and lavender oil;
  • Mix well and pour into a container. Allow it to solidify.

Revitalizing Green Tea Cream

  • 2 tablespoons of green tea-infused oil;
  • 1 tablespoon of shea butter;
  • 1 teaspoon of aloe vera gel;
  • 5 drops of tea tree essential oil.

Instructions:

  • Combine green tea-infused oil and shea butter;
  • Heat until melted, then let it cool slightly;
  • Add aloe vera gel and tea tree oil, mix thoroughly;
  • Pour into a container and allow it to solidify before use.
